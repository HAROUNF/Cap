Our Story

From street culture to premium comfort, discover the journey behind StreetHood

Founded on Passion

StreetHood was born in 2020 from a simple idea: create premium hoodies that blend street culture with everyday comfort. What started as a small collection in a downtown studio has grown into a global brand loved by thousands.

We believe that what you wear should express who you are. Our hoodies aren't just clothing – they're a statement, a lifestyle, a community.
